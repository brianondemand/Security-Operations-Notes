### 7.1 SOAR (Security Orchestration, Automation, and Response)

SOAR is a technology that allows organizations to collect security alerts from different tools, automate repetitive tasks (like phishing email investigation), and power incident response workflows. It helps overwhelmed SOC teams by:

- **Orchestration:** Integrating disparate tools so they can work together.
- **Automation:** Replacing manual, repetitive tasks with machine-driven actions.
- **Response:** Managing incident response processes within a single interface.

### 7.2 AI in the SOC: The Double-Edged Sword

- **AI for Defense:**
    - **Anomaly Detection:** Machine learning models can learn "normal" behavior and flag deviations.
    - **Alert Triage:** AI can help prioritize the most critical alerts, reducing analyst fatigue.
    - **Automated Investigation:** AI can automatically gather context on an alert (e.g., related user, asset, recent activity) before an analyst even looks at it.
      
- **AI-Enabled Attacks:**
    - **Sophisticated Phishing:** Generative AI can create highly convincing, error-free phishing emails in multiple languages.
    - **Evasion:** Adversaries can use AI to test their malware against defensive AI models and modify it to avoid detection.
